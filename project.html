TikTok Redesign



service :

UX Design & Research

started :

Spring 2020 (Original)
10 Feb 2022 (Newest)

completed :

12 Feb 2022

When quarantine hit in Spring 2020, TikTok quickly became popular. With the rise of TikTok, I noticed it was not very accessible. When people would type out their closed captions, it would interfere with the pre-existing caption of the video itself. I had this idea to redesign it and make it more accessible. I started this project in figma, but back then I did not quite know what I was doing, in terms of how to operate figma to redesign. So I decided to focus on the research aspect. It was not until recently I pick up the redesign again and taught myself figma enough to redesign TikTok in 2 days. The results are shown below.
